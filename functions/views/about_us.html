About School On Wheels
School on Wheels is a nonprofit with a large volunteer-driven effort to provide free tutoring and mentoring to children from kindergarten through twelfth grade living in shelters, motels, vehicles, group foster homes, and the streets of Southern California. School on Wheels offers both online tutoring and in-person tutoring. One of its prominent in-person tutoring locations is the Skid Row Learning Center. After school finishes, children enrolled with School on Wheels visit the learning center where they receive a meal, individual tutoring, programmed activities, and playtime. School On Wheels @USC
School on Wheels USC is a student-driven university affiliated organization that partners with School on Wheels with the purpose of mobilizing a community of volunteers and raising greater awareness around education for children at the learning center. The organization organizes carpools between USC and the Skid Row Learning Center as transportation is a hurdle for many volunteers. Furthermore, the organization advertises the opportunity to volunteer at the learning center through word of mouth, social media, and outreach events.
